Over The Garden Wall Fan Page

Parallel 1: The Characters

Wirt and Greg in OTGW mirror Dante and Virgil and even share many of their counterparts' characteristics. Dante is in love with a Beatrice, which is the name of the bluebird in the animated retelling that helps the pair on their journey. Additionally, "the Beast" is an obvious allusion to the Devil.

Parallel 2: Number of Episodes

There are ten episodes in the animated series and ten levels of Hell in the series' literary counterpart. The episodes can be almost directly connected to the events in the book and their related sins.

Parallel 3: The Setting

The boys are lost in the Unknown after an event which is revealed at the end of the series, which can be compared to a sort of Purgatory, much like Dante and Virgil navigating Hell.

Parallel 4: Direct Quotes

The woodsman guards the entrance to Hell and Greg and Wirt are attacked by a dog after hearing haunting words from the woodsman: "Abondon all hope ye who enter here." This is exactly the warning Dante and Virgil see before entering Hell--they are also attacked by animals.
